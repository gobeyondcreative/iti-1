Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund

In light of the recent event surround the Coronavirus outbreak, congress signed the CARES act to help citizens with the undue financial burden caused by the mandated shutdowns. The CARES Act included the Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund (HEERF) in order to provide financial assistance to those students who suffered disruption of their educational delivery due to the mandates.

Our institution has participated in the HEERF program and provides details about its participation below

  • InterAmerican Technical Institute acknowledges that the we signed and returned to the Department the Certification and Agreement and the assurance that the institution intends to use, no less than 50 percent of the funds received under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act to provide Emergency Financial Aid Grants to students. The initial Certification of Agreement was signed and returned to the Department of Education on April 9, 2020.
  • Pursuant to the Certification and Agreement Emergency Financial Aid Grants to students, InterAmerican Technical Institute received $43,606.00 on May 1, 2020 and has one year through May 2, 2021 to distribute the Emergency Financial Aid Grants to students.
  • InterAmerican Technical Institute will start distributing the Emergency Financial Aid Grants to students under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act commencing the week of June 1, 2020 and will update this notification every 45 days thereafter on the amount of Emergency Financial Aid Grants disbursed to students.
  • InterAmerican Technical Institute initially estimates that between 60 to 80 students may be eligible to participate in programs under Section 484 in Title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965 and thus eligible to receive Emergency Financial Aid Grants to students under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act.
  • When InterAmerican Technical Institute starts distributing the Emergency Financial Aid Grant to students under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act, the Institution will update the total number of students who have received the Emergency Financial Aid Grant every 45 days.
  • InterAmerican Technical Institute will determine which students receive Emergency Financial Aid Grants and how much they would receive under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act initially based on students with the lowest Expected Family Contribution (EFC) which demonstrate students with the highest need. All students who filed a FAFSA and enter a payment period (or term) between March 20, 2020 (Campus Closure) and June 1, 2020 (and through June 30, 2020 not to extend over the award year 2019-2020- as guidance provided by the US Department of Education for interruption of study related to Coronavirus-COVID-19) will be eligible to receive the Emergency Financial Aid Grants under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act.
  • Students will receive notification from the Financial Aid Services office notifying them of their eligibility and the process for receiving the Emergency Financial Aid Grants under Section 18004(a)(1) of the CARES Act.
